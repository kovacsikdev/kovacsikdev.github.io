Side Project

Real time map

Tech used: React, Typescript, Node JS, WebSockets

Web app to display a real time map of vehicle locations with their vehicle status. Built on Google Maps, I created 20 bots to accurately simulate 20 individual vehicles driving to their destinations. The UI is device responsive.

Features:
  • Display all 20 bots simultaneously updating their location in a frictionless manner
  • Sortable list of all connected bots
  • Selecting a vehicle will:
    • Zoom in to and follow the vehicle
    • Update the selected vehicle status such as tire pressure
